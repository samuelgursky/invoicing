# srt-tools

## Getting Started

Follow the step-by-step tutorial below to learn how to set up and use the script to edit your existing SRTs in Google Sheets.

### Tutorial Steps

#### Step 1: Create a new document within Google Sheets.
![Step 1](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.55.35%20PM.png)

#### Step 2: In the top bar, click "File" > "Import".
![Step 2](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.55.41%20PM.png)

#### Step 3: Select "Upload" and then "Browse" to select your files.
![Step 3](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.56.02%20PM.png)

#### Step 4: Import the "SRT Tools.xlsx" template downloaded from this Repository.
![Step 4](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%209.01.04%20PM.png)

#### Step 5: I would personally "Replace Current Spreadsheet" but ultimately this is up to you.
![Step 5](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.56.14%20PM.png)

#### Step 6: Here's your template!
![Step 6](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.57.50%20PM.png)

#### Step 7: Select from the top bar "Extensions" > "Apps Script"
![Step 7](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.57.52%20PM.png)

#### Step 8: Here's your Apps Script interface.
![Step 8](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.58.14%20PM.png)

#### Step 9: Navigate to "srt-tools.gs" within this Repository.
![Step 9](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.58.22%20PM.png)

#### Step 10: Copy contents of "srt-tools.gs"
![Step 10](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.58.33%20PM.png)

#### Step 11: Paste within your Apps Script and Save.
![Step 11](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.58.38%20PM.png)

#### Step 12: Click "Run" to initialize your Apps Script.
![Step 12](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.58.55%20PM.png)

#### Step 13: Authorization is required to allow your services within Google to access each other.
![Step 13](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.59.00%20PM.png)

#### Step 14: Review and click "Allow" to continue once you've thoroughly reviewed and understand.
![Step 14](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.59.05%20PM.png)

#### Step 15: Once you see this screen, you're all set and can return to Google Sheets.
![Step 15](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.59.08%20PM.png)

#### Step 16: Refresh and you'll see this new custom menu option.
![Step 16](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.59.22%20PM.png)

#### Step 17: Custom Menu Option - Show Upload Instructions
![Step 17](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%207.59.49%20PM.png)

#### Step 18: Custom Menu Option - Process Uploaded SRTs
![Step 18](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%208.00.00%20PM.png)

#### Step 19: Custom Menu Option - Export Active Sheet as SRT
![Step 19](Images/Screenshot%202024-06-16%20at%208.00.03%20PM.png)
